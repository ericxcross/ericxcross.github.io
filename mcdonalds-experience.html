A new customer journey design to transition McDonalds restaurants to digital (self serve) ordering in Australia.

Freelance Project with Blue Sky for McDonald's Australia

Project: Redesign of Self-Serve customer journey at McDonald's

Role: Research / Wireframing / User Journeys / Customer Flow

Timeline: April - June 2016

UX Solutions

The redesign included a multi-tiered strategy that scaled the experience across a range of restaurant types. A central totem and clear zoning helped direct customers to the appropriate ordering method (self- serve kiosk, staff assisted or at table ordering) and increased usability of the restaurants.

In collaboration with decor designers Juicy Design, this new customer experience strategy was applied to the Castle Hill pilot restaurant in 2017.
