Ellie Pro Chair Family

Client: ITO Design for Profim (Flokk Group)

Involvement: Designer at ITO Design

Launch: November 2018


Ellie is a chair family that responds to the merging traditions of home and office with a soft and inviting character. To meet EN1335 ergonomic standards, we concealed a synchro tilt mechanism under the seat with upholstery, enabling a calm simplicity to the aesthetic rarely seen in ergonomic work chairs.

Four unique base designs and two backrest heights allow these chairs to be placed and enjoyed across a wide range of environments.
