Ellie is a chair family that responds to the merging traditions of home and office with a soft and inviting character. It offers good ergonomics for task oriented work in a wide range of environments.

ITO Design for Profim

Project: Redesign of Self-Serve customer journey at McDonald's

Role: Market Research / Design Strategy / Design Workshops / Product Development

Timeline: April - June 2016

Design Strategy

After a string of recent aquisitions, Flokk, a leading furniture company, tasked ITO Design with determining a new creative strategy for their latest partner, Profim.


Key Insights

  • Office spaces exude an increasingly casual vibe with emphasis on comfort and collaboration
  • Shared spaces and hot-desking mean seating is often shared by multiple users per day.
  • While ergonomics are the focus of task chairs at work, they are often overlooked in home offices.

New Product Requirements

  • Soft and inviting form and limited adjustment without sacrificing ergonomic function.
  • Wide range of design options suitable to both home and office environments
  • EN 1335 Type C Office Furniture Standard
  • Accessible price point

Task Chair Family

The complete family with 4 swivel bases and two backrest heights provide a wide range of options for different settings in the home and office.
