I'm Eric, a Canadian user experience designer based in Adelaide, Australia.

I'm currently working remotely as a UX designer for Money Dashboard, a personal finance management service in the UK.

My previous experience includes designing workplace productivity solutions with ITO Design in Switzerland, imagining new customer experiences at Blue Sky in Australia and designing the future of OLED lighting at OTI Lumionics in Canada.

Before starting my career in design, I earned a Bachelor of Industrial Design from Carleton University, where my thesis project to design a new supply management system for Canadian ICUs was awarded a Provost Scholarship for academic research. I also hold a professional development award in Software Development from Codeclan in Scotland.
