About Me

I'm a Canadian industrial designer based in Adelaide, Australia. I take a usability focused approach to designing new products including ergonomic furniture, healthcare products and consumer electronics

My previous experience includes designing workplace productivity solutions with ITO Design in Switzerland, imagining new customer experiences at Blue Sky in Australia and designing the future of OLED lighting at OTI Lumionics in Canada.

I earned a Bachelor of Industrial Design from Carleton University, where my thesis project to design a new supply management system for Canadian ICUs was awarded a Provost Scholarship for academic research. I also hold a professional development award in Software Development from Codeclan in Scotland.

I'm currently looking for new opportunities in Adelaide.
