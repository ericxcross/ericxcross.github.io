Eric Cross — I'm currently working at Money Dashboard in Edinburgh, UK to make personal finance more open, transparent and accessible for everyone.

My previous experience includes designing workplace productivity solutions with ITO Design in Switzerland, imagining future customer experiences at Blue Sky in Australia and freelancing across multiple companies, countries and time-zones.

Before starting my career in UX, I earned a Bachelor of Industrial Design from Carleton University. My thesis project to design a new supply management system for Canadian ICUs was awarded a Provost Scholarship for academic research.

I've also completed a 16 week intensive software development course at Codeclan (SCQF Level 8 qualification in Sotware Development) to learn industry relevant programming skills and how to work more closely with engineers on product development.

Beyond design, I enjoy running, hiking and camping. If you can't find me, I'm probably on a mountain somewhere.
