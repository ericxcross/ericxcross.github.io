Eric Cross — I'm currently based in Edinburgh, UK, working as a freelance designer on next generation products and services in the ergonomics, healthcare and fintech sectors.

My previous experience includes designing workplace productivity solutions with ITO Design in Switzerland, imagining future customer experiences at Blue Sky in Australia and designing the future of OLED lighting at OTI Lumionics in Canada.

Before starting my career, I earned a Bachelor of Industrial Design from Carleton University. My thesis project to design a new supply management system for Canadian ICUs was awarded a Provost Scholarship for academic research.

I'm also well versed in computational design using Grasshopper and Python, and have consulted on industry projects to design textures and patterns for ergonomics, material reduction and visual effect.
